The most common questions (per group) that comes to a revisor's mind during the reading

So many questions can arise during the reading that sometimes is hard to fully understand the description of the experiment. Frequently, revisors have to send emails in order to contact authors to understand (sometime basics) concepts applied.
It is common have questions such as:

"Which kernel method did they use?", "What is the regularization constant value?", "How many folds were used to cross-validation section?", "Did he normalize the data?", "What is the data distribution?", "Does any hypothesis test has been applied?", "What are the hardware's specifications?"
